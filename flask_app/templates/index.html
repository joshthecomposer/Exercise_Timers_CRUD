This website allows you to create and save multiple exercise interval timers!

All your cardio or HIIT needs can be met. Need a timer for jump rope? Create a timer for jump rope! Wanna do some sprints? Switch to your sprints timer!

When you are exercising, your progress will be saved in the database, so if you switch devices or lose connection, you can pick up your workout where you left off.
